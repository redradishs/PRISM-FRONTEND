About PRISM

Revolutionizing Assessment Management

PRISM is Gordon College's innovative assessment platform, designed to empower educators with intelligent tools for creating, managing, and analyzing student assessments in real-time.

🎯

Our Mission

To revolutionize educational assessment through AI-powered technology, making evaluation more efficient, insightful, and accessible for both educators and students.

🚀

Our Vision

To become the leading assessment platform that transforms how institutions measure and improve student learning outcomes through intelligent automation.

💡

Our Values

Innovation, accessibility, data-driven insights, and commitment to enhancing the educational experience for all stakeholders.
